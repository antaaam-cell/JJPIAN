Chapter One

Our Movement's Vision.

We're not here to redesign the prospectus PDF or add another motivational quote to the noticeboard. We're here to ask, in writing, on record — where did our internal marks go, and why does the placement brochure only ever mention one number.

Why we exist

Build a party for every student who's been told to "focus on studies" while the syllabus, the seating chart, and the semester calendar all changed without notice. That's the whole mission. The rest is satire.

What we're not

Not a rival to the Student Council. Not funded by a coaching institute. Not interested in your CGPA. Just a very loud, very organized group of students asking for paperwork to match promises.

The Five Demands

The Manifesto.

Filed like an exam paper, because that's the only format the system seems to follow.

JJP / Charter of Demands
Total Marks: 5 · Time: Indefinite
Q.01

If JJP forms the student council, every internal assessment answer script shall be optional to the students to attend, according to their mood — not just on request, by default.

Non-negotiable
Q.02

The Placement Cell shall publish the unemployment rate alongside the placed student list on every brochure, poster, and Instagram post.

Full disclosure
Q.03

Any revaluation request shall be resolved within 15 working days, not "next semester, maybe."

Time-bound
Q.04

Hostel and mess fee hikes require a student council vote before implementation — a WhatsApp broadcast is not consent.

Consent required
Q.05

Class timetables and faculty allocations shall be published according to students' choice not by college authority, for every batch.

Membership

Are you eligible to join?

We don't check your branch, your batch, or your attendance percentage. We do have four standards.

REQ / 01

Currently Enrolled

Any UG, PG, diploma, or PhD student across any JIS Group institute. Alumni welcome as advisors.

REQ / 02

Backlog Haver (optional)

Having a backlog is fully respected here, never required, and never held against you.

REQ / 03

Canteen Regular

Working knowledge of the canteen menu and its ever-changing prices strongly preferred.

REQ / 04

Hostel Wi-Fi Survivor

Must have personally experienced the hostel Wi-Fi buffering during a live viva at least once.

Membership is free, campus-wide, and revocable only by you. No fees. No forwarding this to 10 groups.
